Air Travel

There are daily flights from/to Boise. Please see this list of non-stop destinations to Boise, which includes several international hubs (e.g. Seattle, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Chicago, Minneapolis). For those traveling internationally and/or light, it is sometimes less expensive to fly to one of these hubs and then get round trip tickets to Boise from/to that hub.

 

Ground Transportation

There is free transportation to the conference hotels:

  • The Hampton Inn & Suites Boise Downtown provides a complimentary shuttle bus
  • The Inn at 500 provides a complimentary luxury shuttle service

Alternatively, you can take a taxi or use Uber or Lyft. The airport is only 10-15 minutes from the downtown area.

 

Parking at JUMP

Paid parking is available on the 3rd and 4th levels of the JUMP parking garage or in Simplot’s below ground parking garage, both accessible from the Myrtle Street entrance located between 11th and 9th Streets. Please note, the below ground parking garage is only available after 4PM until 6AM on weekdays and all day on weekends. Credit/debit cards accepted only. More information can be found at Parking.

Boise

Boise rests in a beautiful pocket of Idaho. Its outdoor attractions stir the interest of nature-loving people. The scenery provides many attractions for visitors, but the city also contains a diverse and lively cast of shops and events. Downtown Boise is filled with bustling markets, restaurants, and night spots. Boise is also home to the Boise State University Broncos. Over 24,000 students populate the area, creating an energetic and active atmosphere.

 

 

You can learn more about Downtown Boise at https://downtownboise.org, and the City of Boise, at https://www.cityofboise.org/.

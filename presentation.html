Presentations

Oral Presenations

20 minutes plus 5 minutes for questions.

Please bring your own laptop for presentations, or bring it on a USB stick. The projector will use a 16:9 format, so it is recommended (but not required) to use this aspect ratio for your slides, in order to maximize your presentation. If you use your own laptop, make sure you have an HDMI connection, or an adapter for that. Check your presentation with the session chair during the break before.



Posters

Poster boards are 90x190 (w x h). They are folded, forming a "w". We will have two poster presenters per "w". This means that the presenter has two areas of 90 x 190 at their disposal. We recommend a portrait-oriented A0 format.
